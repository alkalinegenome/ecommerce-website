Amethyst

Amethyst is the most precious and valuable stone belonging to the quartz group of minerals, with exception to rare blue-green gem silica. It is recognized as the official birthstone for the month of July. Amethyst belongs to the macrocrystalline branch of quartz and owes its violet-purple color to iron and aluminum impurities.
